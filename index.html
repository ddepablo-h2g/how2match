📖 How to use How2Match
Step-by-step instructions. Select your language below.
ℹ️ What is How2Match?
A B2B matchmaking tool designed for trade fairs and business events. It connects hosted buyers (invited purchasing companies) with exhibitors / sellers (companies showcasing at the event) based on criteria you define — products, markets, languages, etc. The tool builds an optimised meeting schedule and sends each participant their personalised agenda by email. Buyers and sellers are referred to together as participants.
⚠️ Gmail sending limits: Google allows up to 100 emails/hour. For events with 100+ participants, the tool auto-pauses every 50 emails. Do not close the browser tab while sending.

Step 1 — Event setup

1
Enter the event name, organiser and dates.
2
Set available tables (physical meeting spaces at the fair), min meetings (buyers are usually committed to attend a minimum; sellers need a minimum to justify their participation) and max meetings.
3
Enter the expected number of buyers and sellers — the tool will calculate capacity in real time and warn you if the numbers don't add up.
4
Choose Desk mode: Fixed (each buyer keeps the same desk) or Rotating (desks shared per slot — required when buyers > tables).
5
Use the Schedule builder to generate time slots. Set date, start time, end time and any break between meetings. Click Generate. Repeat for afternoon sessions or other days. Use Add break to insert lunch or coffee break rows.

Step 2 — Participant data

1
Download the Buyers template (hosted buyers) and Sellers template (exhibitors). Fill one row per company.
2
Values in matching columns must be comma-separated and spelled identically in both files. Example: Spain, France, Portugal or Apples, Citrus, Berries. A typo or missing comma = no match.
3
You can rename the Criterion 1–5 column headers to match your event (e.g. "Product", "Region", "Certification"). Upload each file — the tool auto-detects your headers.

Step 3 — Matching rules

1
Map buyer columns to seller columns. Example: buyer "Region of interest" ↔ seller "Region of interest".
2
Required: both must share at least one value — if not, no meeting is scheduled. Preferred: boosts the match score when values overlap, but does not block the match.
3
Avoid using numeric ranges (e.g. "€50,000–200,000") as Required criteria — exact text match is very unlikely. Use them as Preferred instead.
4
Click Generate matches.

Step 4 — Review schedule

1
The macro schedule shows all meetings. Hover any cell to see why that buyer–seller pair was matched and which desk they will use.
2
Participants below the minimum appear in the No match tab with a warning.
3
In Fixed mode, go to Desks to assign or edit the fixed desk number for each buyer. Buyers stay at their desk — sellers move between tables.
4
Check Integrity to confirm no double-bookings.
5
Export the macro schedule as CSV for your records. In Fixed mode, the desk appears in each buyer's column header. In Rotating mode, the desk appears in each cell.

Step 5 — Agenda template

1
Upload your event banner image (logo/header).
2
Edit the Buyers version and Sellers version separately. Buyers see "stay at your desk, sellers come to you." Sellers see "move to desk X at each time."
3
Enable the Signature column if you want participants to sign each meeting as proof of attendance.

Step 6 — Send agendas

1
Click Connect Gmail in the top bar and authorise with your Google account.
2
Select the email and name columns from your uploaded files.
3
Enter your own email in the Send test box and click Send test — you will receive 1 buyer agenda and 1 seller agenda to verify format and delivery.
4
Click Generate preview, review the sample agendas, then click Approve to unlock bulk sending.
5
Click Send all agendas. Each participant receives their personalised agenda directly from your Gmail. The tool auto-pauses every 50 emails to respect Gmail limits.
ℹ️ ¿Qué es How2Match?
Una herramienta de matchmaking B2B para ferias y eventos de negocio. Conecta compradores invitados (empresas compradoras que acuden por invitación) con expositores / vendedores (empresas que exponen en la feria) según criterios que tú defines. Genera un calendario optimizado de reuniones y envía a cada participante su agenda personalizada por email. Compradores y vendedores se llaman conjuntamente participantes.
⚠️ Límite de envío Gmail: Google permite hasta 100 emails/hora. Para eventos con más de 100 participantes, la herramienta hace pausas automáticas cada 50 emails. No cierres el navegador durante el envío.

Paso 1 — Configuración del evento

1
Introduce el nombre del evento, organizador y fechas.
2
Configura las mesas disponibles, el mínimo de reuniones (los compradores suelen tener un mínimo comprometido; los vendedores necesitan un mínimo para justificar su participación) y el máximo.
3
Introduce el número esperado de compradores y vendedores — la herramienta calculará la capacidad en tiempo real.
4
Elige el modo de mesa: Fixed (cada comprador tiene mesa fija) o Rotating (mesas compartidas por slot — obligatorio cuando compradores > mesas).
5
Usa el generador de slots: selecciona fecha, hora de inicio y fin, y minutos de descanso entre reuniones. Haz clic en Generar. Repite para bloques de tarde u otros días. Usa Add break para insertar pausas.

Paso 2 — Datos de participantes

1
Descarga las plantillas de Compradores y Vendedores. Rellena una fila por empresa.
2
Los valores en columnas de matching deben separarse con comas y estar escritos idénticamente en ambos archivos. Un error tipográfico = sin match.
3
Puedes renombrar las columnas Criterion 1–5 según tu evento (ej. "Producto", "Región"). Sube cada archivo — la herramienta detecta los headers automáticamente.

Paso 3 — Reglas de matching

1
Conecta columnas de compradores con columnas de vendedores. Ejemplo: "Región de interés" ↔ "Región de interés".
2
Obligatorio: sin valor común = sin reunión. Preferible: mejora el score cuando coincide, pero no bloquea el match.
3
Evita usar rangos numéricos como criterio Obligatorio — úsalos como Preferible.
4
Haz clic en Generar matches.

Paso 4 — Revisar la agenda

1
Pasa el ratón sobre cualquier celda para ver por qué coincidieron y en qué mesa.
2
Los participantes por debajo del mínimo aparecen en la pestaña Sin match.
3
En modo Fixed, asigna mesas en la pestaña Desks. Verifica la pestaña Integrity.
4
Exporta el macro schedule como CSV para tus registros.

Paso 5 — Plantilla de agenda

1
Sube la imagen de cabecera del evento.
2
Edita por separado la versión de Compradores y de Vendedores.
3
Activa la columna Signature si quieres que los participantes firmen cada reunión como prueba de asistencia.

Paso 6 — Enviar agendas

1
Haz clic en Connect Gmail y autoriza con tu cuenta de Google.
2
Selecciona las columnas de email y nombre de cada archivo.
3
Pon tu email en el campo de test y envía — recibirás 1 agenda de comprador y 1 de vendedor para verificar.
4
Genera previsualización, apruébala y haz clic en Enviar todas las agendas. La herramienta hace pausas automáticas cada 50 emails.
ℹ️ How2Match nedir?
Ticaret fuarları için B2B eşleştirme aracı. Hosted alıcıları ile fuarcılar/satıcıları tanımladığınız kriterlere göre eşleştirir. Optimum bir toplantı takvimi oluşturur ve her katılımcıya kişiselleştirilmiş programını e-posta ile gönderir. Alıcılar ve satıcılar birlikte katılımcılar olarak adlandırılır.
⚠️ Gmail gönderim limiti: Google saatte 100 e-postaya izin verir. Araç her 50 e-postada otomatik duraklar. Gönderim sırasında tarayıcıyı kapatmayın.

Adım 1 — Etkinlik kurulumu

1
Etkinlik adını, organizatörü ve tarihleri girin.
2
Mevcut masaları, minimum toplantı sayısını ve maksimum sayıyı ayarlayın.
3
Beklenen alıcı ve satıcı sayısını girin — araç kapasiteyi gerçek zamanlı hesaplar.
4
Masa modunu seçin: Fixed (sabit masa) veya Rotating (slot başına paylaşılan — alıcı > masa ise zorunlu).
5
Program oluşturucuyu kullanarak zaman dilimleri oluşturun. Öğleden sonra veya farklı günler için tekrarlayın. Mola eklemek için Add break'i kullanın.

Adım 2 — Katılımcı verileri

1
Alıcılar ve Satıcılar şablonlarını indirin. Her şirket için bir satır doldurun.
2
Değerler virgülle ayrılmalı ve her iki dosyada aynı şekilde yazılmalıdır. Yazım hatası = eşleşme yok.
3
Criterion 1–5 sütun başlıklarını etkinliğinize göre yeniden adlandırabilirsiniz. Her dosyayı yükleyin.

Adım 3 — Eşleştirme kuralları

1
Alıcı sütunlarını satıcı sütunlarıyla eşleyin.
2

3
Eşleşme oluştur'a tıklayın.

Adımlar 4–6 — Program, Şablon ve Gönderim

1
Programı inceleyin. Hücrelerin üzerine gelerek eşleşme nedenini ve masa numarasını görün.
2
Şablonları düzenleyin. İsterseniz Signature sütununu etkinleştirin.
3
Connect Gmail'e tıklayın, test e-postası gönderin, önizleyin, onaylayın ve Tüm programları gönder'e tıklayın.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q
What are Buyers, Sellers and Participants?
Buyers (hosted buyers) are invited purchasing companies attending the fair with a minimum meeting commitment. Sellers (exhibitors) are companies showcasing their products or services — they need a minimum number of meetings to justify their participation. Together they are called Participants.
Q
What is a Slot?
A time slot is one meeting period — e.g. 9:00–9:20. Each slot can host as many simultaneous meetings as there are tables available.
Q
What is a Break?
A non-meeting period (lunch, coffee) inserted in the schedule. It appears in the macro schedule view but no meetings are assigned to it.
Q
What is a Criterion?
A column used for matching — e.g. "Product category" or "Region of interest". You can rename the Criterion 1–5 columns in the template to reflect your event's specific needs.
Q
Required vs Preferred criteria?
Required: if buyer and seller share no common value in this column, no meeting is scheduled between them. Preferred: shared values increase the match score and prioritise the meeting, but a meeting can still happen without overlap.
Q
Fixed vs Rotating desk mode?
Fixed: each buyer has a permanent desk for the entire event. Sellers move between desks. Use when you have as many tables as buyers.
Rotating: desks are shared per slot — only buyers with a meeting at that time occupy a desk. Required when buyers > tables. Maximises table usage.
Q
What is the Macro Schedule?
The master view for the organiser — slots as rows, buyers as columns, assigned seller in each cell. In Fixed mode the desk appears in each buyer's column header. In Rotating mode it appears in each cell. Export as CSV for your records.
Q
What is Integrity?
The Integrity check verifies that no seller is double-booked (assigned to two meetings in the same slot) and no buyer has the same seller twice.
Q
Can I upload my own Excel without using the template?
Yes, but we strongly recommend working from the provided templates and normalising your data first — consistent spelling, comma-separated values, and matching column names across buyer and seller files. Inconsistent data = missed matches.
Q
A participant has specific availability constraints. What do I do?
The tool does not currently support individual slot exclusions. Handle these adjustments manually after generating the schedule — edit the CSV or the agenda before sending.
Q
Are agendas sent as PDF?
No — agendas are sent as formatted HTML inside the email body. To share a PDF version, export the CSV and format it manually in Google Sheets or Excel.
Q
Gmail sending limits and permissions?
When you click Connect Gmail, you authorise the tool to send emails on your behalf. Google allows up to 100 emails per hour. The tool auto-pauses every 50 emails. Do not close the browser during sending. Your Gmail credentials are never stored — the connection expires when you close the browser.
Q
What about AI matching?
AI matching (Gemini) is coming soon. When active, it will interpret semantically equivalent values across languages — "Cítricos", "Lemon" and "Limón" would all match automatically, eliminating the need for manual data normalisation.
Q
¿Qué son compradores, vendedores y participantes?
Los compradores son empresas compradoras invitadas con un mínimo de reuniones comprometido. Los vendedores son expositores que necesitan un mínimo para justificar su participación. Juntos se denominan participantes.
Q
¿Qué es un slot?
Un período de reunión (ej. 9:00–9:20). En cada slot pueden celebrarse tantas reuniones simultáneas como mesas haya disponibles.
Q
¿Qué es una pausa?
Un período sin reuniones (comida, café). Aparece en el macro schedule pero no se le asignan reuniones.
Q
¿Qué es un criterio?
Una columna usada para el matching (ej. "Categoría de producto"). Puedes renombrar las columnas Criterion 1–5 según tu evento.
Q
¿Obligatorio vs Preferible?
Obligatorio: sin valor común = sin reunión. Preferible: valores comunes aumentan el score pero no bloquean la reunión.
Q
¿Fixed vs Rotating?
Fixed: cada comprador tiene mesa fija. Vendedores se mueven. Usa cuando mesas ≥ compradores.
Rotating: mesas compartidas por slot. Obligatorio cuando compradores > mesas. Maximiza el uso del espacio.
Q
¿Qué es el Macro Schedule?
Vista maestra para el organizador — slots en filas, compradores en columnas. En Fixed la mesa aparece en la cabecera de columna. En Rotating aparece en cada celda. Expórtalo como CSV.
Q
¿Qué es la Integridad?
Verifica que ningún vendedor esté reservado doble y que ningún comprador tenga el mismo vendedor dos veces.
Q
¿Puedo subir mi propio Excel?
Sí, pero normaliza los datos antes — ortografía consistente, valores separados por comas, nombres de columnas coincidentes. Datos inconsistentes = matches perdidos.
Q
Un participante no puede en un slot concreto. ¿Qué hago?
La herramienta no admite exclusiones individuales. Ajusta manualmente el schedule tras generarlo.
Q
¿Las agendas se envían como PDF?
No — como HTML en el cuerpo del email. Para PDF, exporta el CSV y fórmatealo en Google Sheets o Excel.
Q
Permisos y límites de Gmail
Connect Gmail autoriza el envío en tu nombre. Máximo 100/hora, pausas cada 50. Las credenciales nunca se almacenan — la conexión expira al cerrar el navegador.
Q
¿Y la IA?
Próximamente. Gemini AI interpretará valores equivalentes entre idiomas — "Cítricos", "Lemon" y "Limón" coincidirán automáticamente.
Q
Alıcı, Satıcı ve Katılımcı nedir?
Alıcılar minimum toplantı taahhüdü olan davetli satın alma şirketleridir. Satıcılar katılımlarını meşrulaştırmak için minimum toplantıya ihtiyaç duyan fuarcılardır. İkisi birlikte katılımcılardır.
Q
Slot nedir?
Bir toplantı zaman dilimi (örn. 9:00–9:20). Her slotta mevcut masa sayısı kadar eşzamanlı toplantı yapılabilir.
Q
Zorunlu ve Tercihli kriter farkı?
Zorunlu: ortak değer yoksa toplantı planlanmaz. Tercihli: ortak değerler skoru artırır ama toplantıyı engellemez.
Q
Fixed ve Rotating farkı?
Fixed: sabit masa. Rotating: masalar slot başına paylaşılır. Alıcı sayısı > masa sayısı ise Rotating zorunludur.
Q
PDF olarak gönderilir mi?
Hayır — e-posta gövdesinde HTML. PDF için CSV indirip Google Sheets'te biçimlendirin.
Q
Gmail izinleri ve limitleri?
Saatte 100 e-posta. Her 50'de otomatik duraklar. Kimlik bilgileri asla saklanmaz — tarayıcı kapatıldığında bağlantı sona erer.
Q
Yapay Zeka eşleştirme?
Yakında. Gemini AI ile "Narenciye", "Lemon" ve "Limón" otomatik eşleşecek — manuel normalleştirmeye gerek kalmayacak.
Q
구매자, 판매자, 참가자란?
구매자는 최소 미팅 약속이 있는 초청 구매 기업입니다. 판매자는 참가를 정당화하기 위한 최솟값이 필요한 전시업체입니다. 둘을 합쳐 참가자라고 합니다.
Q
슬롯이란?
하나의 미팅 시간 단위(예: 9:00–9:20). 각 슬롯에서 테이블 수만큼 동시 미팅이 가능합니다.
Q
필수 vs 선호 기준?
필수: 공통 값 없으면 미팅 없음. 선호: 공통 값이 점수를 높이지만 미팅을 막지 않습니다.
Q
Fixed vs Rotating?
Fixed: 고정 테이블. Rotating: 슬롯별 공유. 구매자 > 테이블이면 Rotating 필수입니다.
Q
PDF로 전송되나요?
아니요 — 이메일 본문에 HTML로 전송됩니다. PDF는 CSV를 내보내 Google Sheets에서 만드세요.
Q
AI 매칭은?
곧 출시. Gemini AI로 "감귤류", "Lemon", "Limón"이 자동 매칭됩니다.
Q
O que são compradores, vendedores e participantes?
Compradores são empresas convidadas com compromisso mínimo de reuniões. Vendedores são expositores que precisam de um mínimo para justificar a participação. Em conjunto chamam-se participantes.
Q
O que é um slot?
Um período de reunião (ex. 9:00–9:20). Cada slot permite tantas reuniões simultâneas quantas mesas disponíveis.
Q
Obrigatório vs Preferível?
Obrigatório: sem valor comum = sem reunião. Preferível: aumenta o score mas não bloqueia.
Q
Fixed vs Rotating?
Fixed: mesa fixa por comprador. Rotating: mesas partilhadas por slot. Obrigatório se compradores > mesas.
Q
As agendas são PDF?
Não — HTML no corpo do e-mail. Para PDF, exporte o CSV e formate no Google Sheets.
Q
Limites e permissões Gmail?
Até 100 e-mails/hora, pausas a cada 50. As credenciais nunca são guardadas — a ligação expira ao fechar o browser.
Q
E a IA?
Em breve. Gemini AI vai interpretar "Cítricos", "Lemon" e "Limón" como equivalentes automaticamente.
ℹ️ What is How2Match?
A B2B matchmaking tool for trade fairs. It connects hosted buyers (invited purchasing companies) with exhibitors / sellers (companies showcasing at the event). Builds an optimised meeting schedule and sends each participant their personalised agenda by email. Buyers and sellers together are called participants.
⚠️ Gmail sending limits: Google allows up to 100 emails/hour. The tool auto-pauses every 50 emails. Do not close the browser tab while sending.

Step 1 — Event setup

1
Enter event name, organiser, dates, tables, min/max meetings and expected buyer/seller counts.
2
Use the Schedule builder to generate time slots. Repeat for afternoon sessions. Use Add break for lunch/coffee rows.

Step 2 — Participant data

1
Download and fill the Buyers and Sellers templates. One row per company.
2
Values must be comma-separated and spelled identically in both files. A typo = no match.

Steps 3–6

1
Add matching rules and click Generate matches.
2
Review the schedule. Hover cells to see match reason and desk number.
3
Edit templates, connect Gmail, generate preview, approve and send.

❓ FAQ

Q
What are Buyers, Sellers and Participants?
Buyers (hosted buyers) are invited purchasing companies. Sellers (exhibitors) are companies showcasing products/services. Together they are Participants.
Q
What is a Slot?
A time slot is one meeting period (e.g. 9:00–9:20). Each slot can host as many simultaneous meetings as tables available.
Q
What is a Break?
A non-meeting period (lunch, coffee) inserted in the schedule. Appears in the macro view but no meetings are assigned.
Q
What is a Criterion?
A column used for matching (e.g. "Product category", "Region"). Rename the Criterion 1–5 columns in the template to match your event.
Q
Required vs Preferred criteria?
Required: no common value = no meeting scheduled. Preferred: common values boost the match score but don't block the meeting.
Q
Fixed vs Rotating desk mode?
Fixed: each buyer has a permanent desk. Sellers move. Use when tables ≥ buyers.
Rotating: desks are shared per slot — only buyers with a meeting occupy a desk. Required when buyers > tables.
Q
What is the Macro Schedule?
The master organiser view — slots as rows, buyers as columns, assigned seller in each cell. Export as CSV for records.
Q
What is Integrity?
Checks that no seller is double-booked in the same slot and no buyer has the same seller twice.
Q
Can I upload my own Excel?
Yes, but normalise your data first — consistent spelling, comma-separated values, matching column names in both files. Inconsistent data = missed matches.
Q
A participant has slot constraints. What do I do?
The tool does not support individual slot exclusions. Adjust the schedule manually after generating — edit the CSV or agenda before sending.
Q
Are agendas sent as PDF?
No — agendas are sent as HTML in the email body. For PDF, export the CSV and format it in Google Sheets or Excel.
Q
Gmail permissions and limits?
Connect Gmail authorises the tool to send on your behalf. Google allows 100 emails/hour. The tool auto-pauses every 50. Credentials are never stored — the connection expires when you close the browser.
Q
What about AI matching?
Coming soon. When active, Gemini AI will interpret equivalent values across languages — "Cítricos", "Lemon" and "Limón" will match automatically, eliminating manual normalisation.

❓ Preguntas frecuentes

Q
¿Qué son compradores, vendedores y participantes?
Los compradores son empresas compradoras invitadas. Los vendedores son expositores. Juntos son los participantes.
Q
¿Qué es un slot?
Un período de reunión (ej. 9:00–9:20). En cada slot pueden celebrarse tantas reuniones simultáneas como mesas disponibles.
Q
¿Qué es una pausa?
Un período sin reuniones (comida, café). Aparece en el macro schedule pero no se le asignan reuniones.
Q
¿Qué es un criterio?
Una columna usada para el matching (ej. "Categoría de producto"). Renombra las columnas Criterion 1–5 según las necesidades de tu evento.
Q
¿Obligatorio vs Preferible?
Obligatorio: sin valor común = sin reunión. Preferible: valores comunes aumentan el score pero no bloquean la reunión.
Q
¿Fixed vs Rotating?
Fixed: cada comprador tiene mesa fija. Rotating: mesas compartidas por slot. Obligatorio cuando compradores > mesas.
Q
¿Qué es el Macro Schedule?
Vista maestra para el organizador — slots en filas, compradores en columnas. Expórtalo como CSV.
Q
¿Qué es la Integridad?
Verifica que ningún vendedor esté reservado doble y que ningún comprador tenga el mismo vendedor dos veces.
Q
¿Puedo subir mi propio Excel?
Sí, pero normaliza los datos previamente — ortografía consistente y valores separados por comas. Datos inconsistentes = matches perdidos.
Q
Un participante no puede en un slot concreto. ¿Qué hago?
La herramienta no admite exclusiones individuales de slots. Ajusta manualmente el schedule tras generarlo.
Q
¿Las agendas se envían como PDF?
No — como HTML en el cuerpo del email. Para PDF, exporta el CSV y fórmatealo en Google Sheets o Excel.
Q
Permisos y límites de Gmail
Connect Gmail autoriza el envío en tu nombre. Máximo 100/hora, pausas cada 50. Las credenciales nunca se almacenan.
Q
¿Y la IA?
Próximamente. Gemini AI interpretará valores equivalentes entre idiomas — "Cítricos", "Lemon" y "Limón" coincidirán automáticamente.

Alıcı, Satıcı, Katılımcı nedir?

Slot nedir?
Bir toplantı zaman dilimi (örn. 9:00–9:20). Her slotta mevcut masa sayısı kadar eşzamanlı toplantı olabilir.

Zorunlu vs Tercihli kriter?

❓ 자주 묻는 질문

Q
구매자, 판매자, 참가자란?
구매자는 초청 구매 기업, 판매자는 전시업체입니다. 둘을 합쳐 참가자라고 합니다.
Q
슬롯이란?
하나의 미팅 시간 단위(예: 9:00–9:20). 각 슬롯에서 테이블 수만큼 동시 미팅 가능합니다.
Q
필수 vs 선호 기준?
필수: 공통 값 없으면 미팅 없음. 선호: 점수를 높이지만 미팅을 막지 않습니다.
Q
Fixed vs Rotating?
Fixed: 고정 테이블. Rotating: 슬롯별 공유. 구매자 수 > 테이블 수이면 Rotating 필수입니다.
Q
PDF로 전송되나요?
아니요 — 이메일 본문에 HTML로 전송됩니다. PDF는 CSV를 내보내 Google Sheets에서 만드세요.
Q
AI 매칭은?
곧 출시. Gemini AI로 "감귤류", "Lemon", "Limón"이 자동 매칭됩니다.
